What is SOTLive? ▼
SOTLive is a media aggregator that surfaces upcoming sports games and newly released TV shows and movies across multiple streaming and broadcast platforms — all in one place, so you never miss what's on.
What soccer leagues do you cover? ▼
We currently cover the following English language broadcasts in the United States of America:
  • UEFA Champions League
  • UEFA Europa League
  • UEFA Europa Conference League
  • Premier League
  • Major League Soccer (MLS)
  • CONCACAF Champions Cup
  • US Open Cup
  • English FA Cup
  • EFL Championship
  • Serie A
  • German Bundesliga
  • La Liga
  • Dutch Eredivisie
  • USL Championship
  • USL League One
What basketball do you cover? ▼
Currently, we cover men's college basketball — including the NCAA Tournament and NIT. Additional coverage may be added in the future.
Why are some games no longer showing for today? ▼
The schedule refreshes every few hours. Once a game is confirmed over and well in the past, it rolls off the page — we keep things focused on what's upcoming, currently live, or recently finished. If something seems to have disappeared, it's likely already wrapped up.
